The real problem

The problem in this case is extremism and people not educating themselves on how our economy works. The U.S. runs of a specific type of capitalism called Oligarchic Capitalism. In this type, a small group of capitalists have taken over both the economic system and the political system. Because of this, the people at the top, called the Oligarchs, can bend and manipulate the laws and the different systems the country runs on for their own gain. because of this, their own personal goals may not line up with everyone elses goals, causing political extremism. Russia also runs of of this type of caplitalism, however it is far more advanced than ours.

The solution

To solve this, we need to change the type of capitalism our country runs on to something else. Many people think that we need to change the way our country works, we will become socialist. This isn't true, however things would change. If we change our economy from Oligarchic to either Welfare based or Resource based, meaning we implment either fringe benifits or benifits based off of the resources found locally, healthcare would greatly improve. Both of these, however, mean that citizens would have to pay a bit more towards healthcare, around 18%. Even though it is a bit more, paying around 6% more in taxes is a lot better than even more crushing debt on your shoulders.
